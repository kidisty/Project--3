About Our Project

The TripleK Lego Set Decider tool will help users decide what sets they would be interested in based on 5 seperate criterias (piece count, difficulty, rating, year produced, and cost). Our tool will give the user an option of five different sets that are the "nearest neighbor" for the users input. The user can utilize our tool to either combine different sets to build a new model (MOC "my own creation") or purchse sets similar to their own and follow building instructions.

Lego world (what are Legos?)

Legos are a world renowned interlocking plastic bricks, parts and minifies that are used to assemble many different toys. Each set of a Lego includes bricks of various sizes, shapes and colors that can be constructed in many ways. The Lego Group creates new sets with new themes every year as well as invents new shapes of bricks with cool decorations.

The Lego fanbase is huge and these fans interact online to share opinions about LEGO sets, exchange minifigures and discuss about investing in collectible toys of movies, limited seasonal editions, make recommendations to each other about sets to purchase and share ideas about sorting and storing bricks efficiently. Our recommendation tool will also help the lego fans make better purchasing decisions and inspire them to create new assembly ideas.
